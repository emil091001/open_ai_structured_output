Product
Product description:
Troja Original S is a walking aid that helps people with different physical disability stay active
and independent. With two grips and four wheels it provides support while moving around in-
or outdoors. The rollators come in different sizes, height and width, for different surroundings
and needs.
The Troja Original rollator is a strong lightweight rollator with a weight bearing capacity up to
165 kg.
The main parts of the rollators consist of frame, handles and cross in aluminium and glass fibre
reinforced plastic (GFRP) and wheels in GFRP as well as PP, POM, PU and TPE plastic. Additional
parts include breaks, basket, seats and bolts. The basket is an optional add-on.
Product specification:
This EPD is valid for the three sizes of the Troja Original model: XS, S and M. The same materials
are used for all three sizes, but the amounts differ slightly. The material inventory for all sizes is
shown below. The results shown are for the S size without basket. Results for Troja Original XS
and M, as well as the results for the basket add-on are shown under “Additional results”.

LCA: Calculation rules
Declared unit:
1 piece of Troja Original, modules A1-A3, A4, C1-C4 and D
Data quality:
Data is collected during 2023 and is representative for the year 2022 (yearly average). Data for
raw material inputs, manufacturing of the product and transport to market (A1-A3 and A4) is
based on specific data provided by Topro and technical data sheets from the suppliers. End of
Life (C1-C4) scenarios are uncertain and conservative assumptions have therefore been applied.
Generic data for background processes has been modelled using ecoinvent 3.9.1.
Characterization factors from EN15804: 2012 + A2: 2019.
Allocation:
The allocation is made in accordance with the provisions of EN 15804. Incoming energy and
water and waste production in-house is allocated equally among all products through mass
allocation. Effects of primary production of recycled materials allocated to the main product in
which the material was used.
System boundary:
The system boundary is from cradle to gate with options, A1-A3, A4, C1, C2, C3, C4 and D. The
flow chart for production, transport and end of life is shown in the figure below.
Cut-off criteria:
All major raw materials and all the essential energy is included. The production process for raw
materials and energy flows that are included with very small amounts (<1%) are not included.
This cut-off rule does not apply for hazardous materials and substances.
